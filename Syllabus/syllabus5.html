credits to be initiated at BCA w.e.f. the session 2018 – 21. The proposed system may be
modified/improved in future according to the requirements.

Unit 3:

structural metrics – path products and path expressions.

Verification and validation – fundamental tools – levels of testing – testing approaches – types of
testing – test plan – software testing tools: WinRunner – Silk test.
